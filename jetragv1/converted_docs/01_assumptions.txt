# Mo Ambulance – Assumptions & Definitions

Mo Ambulance
A | DEFINITION OF REVENUE SOURCE | DESCRIPTIONS
1 | B2B | Hospital, Industry, Corporates
2 | HAMS | Hospital Ambulance Management System
3 | MHU | Mobile Health Unit
4 | AMTeK & AMTeK-V | Oxygen Sylinder Sensor, Breath Analyser, GPS, web Camera, E-log Book, Dashboard & Vital Parameter device (ECG, Oxygen Saturation, PR, Temp, RR, BP)
6 | ATOMS | Ambulance Traffic Organisation & Management System for Urban and Smart City
6 | KIOSK (PRE PAID) | Pre Paid KISOK for Ambulance Management at Multispeciality Hospital
7 | INDUSTRY | Colloboration Approach in Industry and Corporate for  Ambulance 24X7 Support Service
8 | WHITE LABELLING (B2B) | Labelling of all app (B2C/DRIVER/HAMS) IN HOSPITAL NAME
B | ASSUMPTIONS & CLARIFICATIONS | DESCRIPTIONS
1 | PRE PAID KISOK AMBULANCE MODEL | Mo Ambulance has started the discussion with Odisha Govt. to introduce this Model in Major Hospital with >500 bedded (Medical College & Hospital)
2 | HAMS (Hospital Ambulance Management System | Mo Ambulance has Started the Implementation of Ambulance Support through Integration of tech Application with different Customize Model for Hospital
3 | AMBULANCE OXYGEN SENSOR (B2B) | Mo Ambulance has planned to integrate in all Ambulance of Govt to Monitor the Oxygen Level
4 | Govt. Level Policy Level | Mo Ambulance will have proposal to work with Govt. at the Policy Level implementation on Quality Assurance of the Private ambulance
5 | Smart City Implementation (ATOMS) | Mo Ambulance has planned to introduce the smart city Ambulance Surveillance Model in Bhubaneswar Smart City to avoid the Crowd
6 | IOT | Mo Ambulance is in process to scale up the Bihar Project to State wise
7 | 5G Mo Ambulance | Reformation of Existing Ambulance to 5G Ambulance with Low Cost
8 | Policy Level Implementation of Factory Act 1948 | To implement the inhouse Ambulance as per Factory Act 1948
9 | INDUSTRY | Mo Ambulance has submitted the Proposal to TATA, JSW, PPL for Smart Ambulance Management Project
10 | WHITE LABELLING (B2B) | Mo Ambulance is in Process of Developing the White Labelling Application for Chain of Hospital as Emergency Medical Service App. which includes the Patient App, Ambulance Driver app, and Hospital App.
11 | Insurance Support through B2C App. | Mo Ambulance is in discussion with Insurance Company to support insurance for the Ambulance Ride Cost and also approval of Patient Treatment Cost before arrival at the hospital
12 | Mobile Health Unit (MHU) | MHU support to the Onboarded Hospital for Patient footfall support and revenue generation of both
13 | IOT-Ambulance (Intrapartum Monitoring) | Mo Ambulance will pilot the Project on 102/Janani Express Ambulance for Pregnant women Transportation to the Hospital
14 | Implementation of Universal HAMS | HAMS will be Used by each hospital for Proper Monitoring of the In & out of Ambulance without any delay
15 | Rental Ambulance | As we have the aggregator of Ambulance , we are getting Proposal for Rental Ambulance
